Are you a talented vocalist? Have experience singing choral music? Want the experience of performing challenging yet fun repertoire? Want the experience of performing on the NAC stage?

The Capital Chamber Choir is holding auditions for singers of all voice types who are looking to join our fun-filled ensemble for the 2015-2016 season!

Interested? Please send an email to info@capitalchamberchoir.ca with your voice type and audition sessions you can attend. If you are unable to attend one of the listed audition sessions, an alternative time can be arranged.

Audition location

St. Joseph's Parish (151 Laurier Ave East)

Audition requirements

The audition will consist of the following elements:

  • Prepared Solo: You are asked to prepare an excerpt of a vocal selection of your choice. This selection may be a solo or a choral piece. It may be performed a cappella or with piano accompaniment. An accompanist will be provided at no expense for the audition. Should you wish to rehearse with the accompanist before the audition, the choir manager will provide you with the accompanistâ€™s email.
  • Sight Reading: You will be asked to sight-read a short excerpt. The expectation is that this excerpt will be performed with correct pitch, rhythm, dynamics, and text. This portion of the audition will not be accompanied; however a tuning note will be given.
  • Ear Training: You will be asked to aurally identify various chord qualities (major, minor etc.) and basic intervals.
