The Capital Chamber Choir's 2013-14 season will be our busiest and most exciting yet! We will be performing programmes of diverse repertoire, collaborating with other local ensembles and musicians, and, as always, showcasing some of the finest music Canada (and Ottawa!) has to offer! Be sure to mark your calendars!

Songs of Remembrance

Sunday, November 3, 2013, 3:30pm
St. Joseph's Church (Cumberland St. and Wilbrod St., Ottawa)

The Capital Chamber Choir will join the Ottawa Regional Youth Choir for this concert featuring Faure's Requiem.

Requiem et Lux

Friday, December 6, 2013, 8:00pm
Knox Presbyterian Church (120 Lisgar St., Ottawa)

As we look forward to the winter solstice and the holiday season, this concert will mark the transition from darkness to light with works by Gjeilo, Howells, Lauridsen, Whitacre, and Canadians Glick, Matthews, Morlock, and Togni.

Schnittke Concerto

Saturday, March 22, 2014
St. Joseph's Church (Cumberland St. and Wilbrod St., Ottawa)

The Ewashko Singers, Calixa-Lavalee Choir and Capital Chamber Choir will come together for this concert featuring Schnittke's choral concerto.
