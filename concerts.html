The Capital Chamber Choir is starting its 2016-2017 season by embarking on its first ever major recording project, centred on local and Canadian composers, made possible by a grant from the Ontario Arts Council. Look for more information about the release in the new year!

Our season features collaborations with renowned local musical institutions. On November 11, 2016, we will be singing Brahms’ Requiem in support of the Ottawa Regional Youth Choir, and we will return to Southam Hall in May 2017 to perform Mozart’s Requiem with the National Arts Centre Orchestra, under the direction of John Storg√•rds.

Our regular season concerts in December 2016 and March 2017 will continue to offer the best in contemporary choral music.

Finally, in June 2017, the Capital Chamber Choir will take part in Sing Ottawa en choeur, a weekend-long choral celebration in honour of Canada's sesquicentennial.

We're looking forward to this exciting and eventful season, and we hope you'll join us on this journey!
