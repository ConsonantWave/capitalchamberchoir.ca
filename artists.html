Sara Brooks, Artistic Director

A native of Chatham, Ontario, Sara Brooks received a BMus from Acadia University and a MMus in piano performance from the University of Ottawa. While completing her master’s degree, Sara served for two years as both rehearsal pianist and assistant conductor for the University’s Calixa-Lavallee ensemble, under director Laurence Ewashko. After graduating, Sara worked as the Director of Music at All Saints' Anglican Church in downtown Ottawa for three years. In 2009 she was appointed assistant chorus master and children’s chorus master for Opera Lyra, Ottawa. In addition to this, Sara founded the Capital Chamber Choir, a 24-voice ensemble, whose mandate is to promote Canadian Choral repertoire. This ensemble is now in its third full season.

Sara was recently invited to work with the Vancouver Chamber Choir under conductor Jon Washburn, at the 31st annual VCC National Symposium and she will begin doctoral studies in choral conducting at the University of Alberta in September 2012, studying with Dr. Leonard Ratzlaff. She is currently completing a term as Interim Director of Music at the First Unitarian Congregation of Ottawa.
